Why invest in biodiversity?

It's good for the world.
Healthy, biodiverse ecosystems are naturally more resilient to disturbances such as wildfires, drought, and disease.
‍
It's good for business.
To address nature-related risks, investment in biodiversity-uplifting projects local to your supply chain can help secure your bottom line.

To be ahead of regulatory curves.
Forward-looking companies can be proactive in the face of upcoming state-imposed regulatory and reporting practices.

Our solution

We work with you to identify key areas in your supply chain and operations that can benefit from nature-positive uplifts.

Using this information, we provide a portfolio of biodiversity-positive projects local to your areas of interest. Access a digital twin of your portfolio for at-a-glance intelligence of your project's performance.

Project KPIs will be tracked using our AI-based dMRV technology, powered by a combination of satellite and on-ground data.

We bring transparency to your investments through turnkey reporting of species-level and community-level metrics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring trust and transparency to the voluntary biodiversity market in order to protect, sustain, and improve natural ecosystems, for all who depend on them, by providing data-driven ecological monitoring and enrichment services.
‍
We believe that we can learn from the mistakes of the carbon market to bring true restoration and regeneration to natural ecosystems around the world. Using a holistic lens, we find biodiversity to provide a better system of metrics for this than carbon alone.
